uFactory Lite 6 Robot controll

Part of Studyprogramm which is to be offered to the Masters Students of the University of Karlsruhe as a Lab course where Students learn, how to use an Industrial Robot and do intelligent decision making and automation tasks such as this.

Project Overview

The Taks of this Project was to figure out the objects and their positions on the breadboard using a 3D camera

Such that this information could be further processed to make intelligent motion such as sorting or placing in an order

  • Software: Python Software Develpoment Kit from the Maufacturer, Python, VScode
  • Hardware: uFactory Lite 6, Real sense D345 camera
